Math Visualization Prompts

Welcome to the "Draw the Problem" collection. This set of 10 diagrams features intentionally complex, high-level scenarios that often include missing information.

The primary goal is not to solve them.

Instead, we focus on reducing math anxiety by emphasizing drawing, visualization, and interpretation.

These prompts encourage students to practice translating dense technical text into clear geometric or physical concepts.
